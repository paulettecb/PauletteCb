Rules
Speak to "you" and "your dog." The brand is "we."
Sentence case everywhere. ALL-CAPS only for eyebrow labels with wide tracking.
Short, punchy headlines. Benefit-first product lines.
No emoji in copy. The drawn ♥ is a brand mark — not an emoji — used sparingly.
Talk about: materials, craft, durability, and the dog-human bond.
build beautiful,
stay true.
