Small uppercase pill tag — product status (New, Best Seller), categories, or soft labels.

```jsx
<Badge tone="pop">New ♥</Badge>          {/* one hot tag per view */}
<Badge tone="soft">Waterproof</Badge>
<Badge tone="mint">In stock</Badge>
<Badge tone="outline">Small batch</Badge>
```

Tones: `pop` (magenta), `brand` (periwinkle), `soft` (periwinkle tint), `blush`/`mint`/`butter` (pastels), `outline`.
